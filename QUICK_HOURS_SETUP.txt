STEP 1: CREATE NEW SHEET
────────────────────────
1. Open your Google Sheet
2. Click "+" button at bottom
3. Name it: Hours
4. Press Enter

STEP 2: ADD HEADERS (Row 1)
──────────────────────────
A1: Day
B1: Hours

STEP 3: ADD YOUR HOURS (Starting Row 2)
───────────────────────────────────────
Copy and paste this into your Hours sheet:

Day                 | Hours
monday              | 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM
tuesday             | 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM
wednesday           | 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM
thursday            | 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM
friday              | 10:00 AM - 9:00 PM
saturday            | 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
sunday              | Closed

(Or use your own hours)

STEP 4: SAVE & TEST
───────────────────
1. Make sure your sheet is PUBLIC
2. Reload website (Ctrl+Shift+R)
3. Check Footer → Hours section
4. Check Contact page → Opening Hours

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

IMPORTANT RULES:
────────────────
✅ Use lowercase day names: monday (NOT Monday)
✅ Use this format: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM (NOT 10-20 or 10am-8pm)
✅ Include all 7 days (don't skip any)
✅ For closed days, write: Closed (or "By Appointment")
