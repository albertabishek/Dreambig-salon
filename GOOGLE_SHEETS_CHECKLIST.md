# Google Sheets Setup Checklist

Follow this checklist step-by-step to set up your Google Sheets correctly.

---

## PART 1: Create the Google Sheet

### Create Sheet
- [ ] Go to Google Sheets (sheets.google.com)
- [ ] Click "+ Create"
- [ ] Click "Blank spreadsheet"
- [ ] A new sheet opens

### Name Your Sheet
- [ ] Click "Untitled spreadsheet" at the top left
- [ ] Type: `Salon Services & Gallery` (or your name)
- [ ] Press Enter

### Get Your Sheet ID
- [ ] Look at the browser URL
- [ ] Copy the long ID between `/d/` and `/edit`
- [ ] **Save this ID** - you'll need it later
- [ ] Example: `1a2b3c4d5e6f7g8h9i0j1k2l3m4n5o6p7q8r9s0t`

---

## PART 2: Create Sheet Tabs

### Rename "Sheet1" to "Services"
- [ ] Right-click on "Sheet1" tab at the bottom
- [ ] Click "Rename"
- [ ] Type: `Services`
- [ ] Press Enter

### Create "Gallery" Sheet
- [ ] Click the "+" button next to the sheet tabs
- [ ] New sheet appears with default name
- [ ] Right-click on it → "Rename"
- [ ] Type: `Gallery`
- [ ] Press Enter

### Create "Settings" Sheet
- [ ] Click the "+" button again
- [ ] New sheet appears
- [ ] Right-click on it → "Rename"
- [ ] Type: `Settings`
- [ ] Press Enter

### Verify Sheet Tabs
- [ ] You now have exactly 3 tabs: `Services`, `Gallery`, `Settings`
- [ ] All names are spelled correctly
- [ ] All names have correct capitalization

## PART 6: Share Your Sheet (CRITICAL!)

### Open Share Dialog
- [ ] Click the "Share" button (top right corner)
- [ ] A share dialog opens

### Change Sharing Settings
- [ ] Click "Change to anyone with the link" (or current sharing option)
- [ ] Select access level: **"Viewer"** (read-only)
- [ ] Click "Copy link" to copy the share URL
- [ ] Click "Share" or "Done"

### Verify Public Access
- [ ] Sheet is now public
- [ ] Anyone with the link can VIEW (but not edit)
- [ ] Access level is "Viewer"

### Save the Share Link
- [ ] Copy the share link (looks like): `https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/{SHEET_ID}/edit?usp=sharing`
- [ ] Save it for your developer

---

## PART 7: Prepare Information for Developer

Gather the following information to give to your developer:

### Information to Collect
- [ ] **SHEET_ID**: The ID from your URL (e.g., `1a2b3c4d5e6f7g8h9i0j...`)
- [ ] **Public Share Link**: The full link you copied (e.g., `https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/{ID}/edit?usp=sharing`)
- [ ] **Sheet Names**: Confirm they are: `Services`, `Gallery`, `Settings`

### What to Send to Developer
```
Hi, here's my Google Sheets information:

SHEET_ID: 
[Copy your SHEET_ID here]

Public Share Link: 
[Paste share link here]

Sheet Names:
- Services (with columns: Name, Price, Duration, Category, Description, Image URL)
- Gallery (with columns: Title, Image URL, Description)
- Settings (with columns: Setting, Value)

Ready to connect!
```

## Common Issues Checklist

### Issue: Website shows default/demo data, not mine
- [ ] Is sheet public? (check sharing settings)
- [ ] Are sheet names exactly: Services, Gallery, Settings?
- [ ] Are column headers spelled correctly?
- [ ] Did you give developer the SHEET_ID?

### Issue: Prices or duration show as blank
- [ ] Are they NUMBERS only? (not "$" or "min" text)
- [ ] Are they in correct columns? (B for price, C for duration)
- [ ] Are they in rows 2 and below (not in header)?

### Issue: Gallery images don't appear
- [ ] Are Image URLs complete? (start with https://)
- [ ] Try opening URL in new tab - image should load
- [ ] Are they in column B of Gallery sheet?

### Issue: Services show but categories don't filter
- [ ] Do categories EXACTLY match? (Hair, not "hair" or "HAIR")
- [ ] Are they in column D?
- [ ] No extra spaces?

### Issue: Can't find SHEET_ID
- [ ] Look at your sheet URL
- [ ] Between `/d/` and `/edit` is your ID
- [ ] Example: `docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1a2b3c.../edit`
