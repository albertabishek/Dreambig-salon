## Customize the Examples

### To use your own salon information:

1. **Replace names**: Change "Luxury Salon" to your salon name
2. **Replace phone**: Change "+1 (555) 123-4567" to your phone number
3. **Replace address**: Change "123 Beauty Lane" to your address
4. **Replace prices**: Change prices to match your rates
5. **Replace durations**: Change times to match your service times
6. **Replace descriptions**: Use your own service descriptions
7. **Replace images**: Use your own photos (must be complete URLs)

### For descriptions, keep them short like:

✓ "Professional haircuts tailored to your style"
✓ "Rejuvenating facial for all skin types"
✓ "Relaxing massage to ease tension"

✗ "We provide hair cutting services and we use the best products..."
✗ "Very long description with too many words..."

### For images, use free sources:

- **Unsplash**: unsplash.com (search for "salon", "hair", "spa")
- **Pexels**: pexels.com (search for "beauty", "salon", "massage")
- **Pixabay**: pixabay.com (search for "treatment", "nails", "facial")

## Step-by-Step Paste Instructions

### Method 1: Paste into Google Sheets (Easiest)

1. Copy the table from above
2. Open your Google Sheet
3. Click cell A1
4. Right-click → "Paste special" → "Paste values"
5. Data automatically fills the sheet!

### Method 2: Paste into text editor first

1. Copy the table
2. Paste into Google Docs
3. Copy from Google Docs
4. Paste into Google Sheets
5. Format as needed

### Method 3: Type manually

1. If copy-paste doesn't work, type the headers first
2. Then enter each service manually
3. Takes longer but works reliably
