User visits your website:
1. Browser sends request to website

5. Parse functions convert CSV to TypeScript objects
6. parseHoursData() creates OpeningHours object
7. Hours merged into SalonSettings object
8. PageData returned with services, gallery, settings
9. Navbar rendered with settings.name
10. Footer rendered with settings + settings.openingHours
11. Services page shows settings.services
12. Gallery page shows settings.gallery
13. Contact page shows settings + settings.openingHours
14. User sees everything from YOUR Google Sheet!
