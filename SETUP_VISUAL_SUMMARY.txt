────────────────────────

──────────────────

A6: friday        B6: 10:00 AM - 9:00 PM
A7: saturday      B7: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Step 4: Save
──────────
Ctrl+S (auto-saves in Google Sheets)

Step 5: Test
──────────
Website → Ctrl+Shift+R → Check Footer → See Hours!
